1. Presentation

Our project is a boat bus on the Seine, the boat would serve several stops on the water, this would: - Offer new public transport in the Paris region that can circulate in the capital. - Discover the capital from a new angle - Decongest already saturated transport. Our project aims to be part of a sustainable development perspective. It promotes innovative maritime transport, respecting nature and cleaning up the waters of Paris. Indeed, this boat will be able to pick up the waste on the water thanks to a mechanical "arm".
